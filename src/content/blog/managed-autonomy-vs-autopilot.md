Pure autonomous systems can move quickly, but they often fail on trust, auditability, and policy controls.

Team Orchestrator is built around **managed autonomy**:

- Athena recommends and coordinates.
- Specialists execute.
- Humans approve, intervene, and redirect at any step.

That loop is the core of safe velocity for engineering organizations.
